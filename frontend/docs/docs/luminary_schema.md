Luminary expressions represent periods of highest creative coherence and contribution.

## Luminary Definition
A Luminary is not a role or title. It is a conditional expression state.

## Required Fields
- luminaryId
- name
- activationCondition
- expressionState
- creativeContribution
- integrationRequirement

## Constraints
- Luminaries are not constant.
- They require integration to remain stable.
- Multiple luminary expressions may exist.

## Usage
- Displayed after Shadow interpretation.
- Framed as potential, not destiny.
