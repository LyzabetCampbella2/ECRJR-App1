# ECRJR — Legendary Raveliquars

Legendary Raveliquars are the highest-order identities revealed by the ECRJR main test.

A Raveliquar is not assigned or selected.
It is named through Raveliquarith judgment after the integration of shadow aspects, luminary aspects, and the artist archetype.

## Properties
- Mythic and narrative-grade
- Stable yet evolving
- Singular in identity, infinite in variation
- Carries world, story, and magic logic

## Expression
Each Legendary Raveliquar is expressed as a unique name formed through structured synthesis rather than categorization.

## Role
Legendary Raveliquars represent the creative legend an individual inhabits within their world.
## Canonical Naming Form
Legendary Raveliquars are named using the structure:
“The [Core Title] of [Domain]”.
