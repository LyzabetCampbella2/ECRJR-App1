User interaction flows through the system in the following order:

1. Foundational Tests
2. Shadow Aspects (5)
3. Luminary Aspects (5)
4. Artist Archetype
5. Narrative Axes (5)
6. Legendary Raveliquar Name
7. Legend Narrative
8. Result Page Rendering

No stage may be skipped, duplicated, or reordered.
## Stage Responsibilities

Foundational Tests:
- Collect responses
- Produce raw signals
- Do not interpret meaning

Shadow & Luminary Aspects:
- Represent pressure and coherence states
- Are selected, not evaluated

Artist Archetype:
- Defines form of expression
- Does not assign identity

Narrative Axes:
- Describe tension and movement
- Do not resolve outcomes

Raveliquarith:
- Interprets all prior structures
- Produces name, legend, and judgment
- Is the sole interpretive authority

Frontend:
- Renders content
- Does not compute meaning
- Does not compare results
